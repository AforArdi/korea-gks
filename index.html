Program Overview

The Global Korea Scholarship (GKS) is designed to provide international students with opportunities to study at higher educational institutions in Korea for undergraduate and graduate degrees. Its primary goal is to promote international exchange in education and mutual friendship between countries.

Is IELTS or TOPIK necessary?

Technically, No. The guidelines state they are "Optional". However, applicants with valid TOPIK (Korean proficiency) or English proficiency certificates (IELTS, TOEFL) receive extra points in the evaluation.

Recommendation: Strongly advised if you want to be competitive.
